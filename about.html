About CineMood

Finding the perfect movie for your mood, powered by AI.

The Problem

Ever spent 30 minutes scrolling through Netflix, only to rewatch The Office again? We've all been there. With thousands of movies available, choice paralysis is real. You know you want to watch something—but what matches how you're feeling right now?

Our Solution

CineMood solves this by understanding your mood. Just tell us how you feel— "I need a good laugh," "something thrilling," or "I want to cry"—and our AI instantly predicts the perfect genre for you. No endless scrolling. No regrets.
